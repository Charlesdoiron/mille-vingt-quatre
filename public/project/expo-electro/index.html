Expo Electro2019

Architectural, Scenography & Installation

  • Core

Philharmonie de Paris presents a major exhibition on electronic music through the exploration of its imagination, its innovations, its mythologies and its correspondences with visual arts


Laurent Garnier created several mixes for the exhibition that echo throughout the whole exhibition space. Spanning different eras and places of electronical music, which represents and corresponds to the various areas of the exhibition, dedicated to the history, future and love of electronical music.
The various mixes are also visualized by 1024‘s light installation CORE located in the center of the exhibition space.

In the heart of the exhibition you find the premiere exhibition of 1024’s audiovisual light-installation CORE.

CORE  invites the audience to step into an immersive black box and experience a realtime, volumetric, 3-dimensional light show that embodies Laurent Garniers mixes that echo through the whole exhibition space.

Concluding each of Laurent’s different mixes, is the 1024 produced audiovisual track REBOOT played out in the whole space and visualized by CORE.
